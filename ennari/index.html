What is Ennari?

We are a team dedicated to digital marketing through social media management. Our main objective is to boost the growth of projects and ventures through the creation of digital content that transmits a clear and friendly relationship with the user and meets the needs of each client, depending on the type of venture to which it is focused.

How do we work?

Analysis and objectives: We will begin by analyzing the type of project, the products and/or services offered and we will establish short and long-term objectives.

Strategic plan: A strategic plan will be drawn up according to the results of the analysis and a calendar will be drawn up establishing the distribution and type of content to be used during a given period (Monthly - Quarterly - Semi-annually).

Implementation: the creation of digital content, approval of these, and publication will be carried out.
